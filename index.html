Sistem Aluminium, has started to produce aluminum profiles in 1994.
Sistem Aluminum is an integrated plant which produces over 11.000 products.
Company allocates a large part of its production capacity for exportation in 17 countries. From 2018 on the Georgian market. LTD Alutech Georgia is an exclusive partner of Sistema Aluminium in Georgia.
Sistema Aluminium has ISO 9001:2008, ISO 14001:2004, OHSAS 18001:2007 and ISO/IEC 27001 certificates
